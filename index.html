Doom Hex: Escape
TRAPPED! As a legendary hero, you've been in your fair share of rough spots. But when you went after the ancient Lich, Duhrigol, in the Tombs of Shadow, you nearly met your match. As you struck forth with the killing blow, the dying Lich cast his most powerful spell: the Doom Hex.

Although you managed to destroy Duhrigol, the Hex transported you to his underground dungeon. Even worse, you were afflicted by it's terrible curse: you will grow weaker and weaker as you spend more time in the dungeon!

You must escape...or die.
